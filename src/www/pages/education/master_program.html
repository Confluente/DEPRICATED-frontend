Honors for Master students

The Graduate School has developed an exciting honors program for excellent Master students. By joining the Honors Academy in the Graduate School you will get the chance to explore and enhance your personal leadership skills in a professional context.

You can opt for a focus on ‘excellence for science’, ‘excellence for society’ or ‘excellence for industry’. Within this focus you can put a personal emphasis, for example on research, design, entrepreneurship, teaching, management or a combination.

Regardless of your chosen focus and emphasis this program will give you the opportunity to:

  • define and achieve your own ambitious challenges (personal leadership)
  • build your own program suited to your own interests and needs
  • prepare yourself for scientific, societal and/or industrial leadership
  • focus on the outside world in terms of contents and people
  • give yourself a head start for your future career and job opportunities
