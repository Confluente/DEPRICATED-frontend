Connecting Honors students

Welcome to H.S.A. Confluente

H.S.A. Confluente is the study association of the Honors Academy, it is the place where all students from different tracks come together to enjoy both educative and fun activities. You start off with a great, once in a life time experience during the introduction weekend, where you meet all your fellow honors students. H.S.A. Confluente focuses on the curiosity of the students by offering a wide range of workshops; from dancing to CV workshops to company interviews. Next to that, H.S.A. Confluente also organizes network events and other events in collaboration with honors students from all over the Netherlands .
