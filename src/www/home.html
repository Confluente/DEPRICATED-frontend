H.S.A. Confluente aims to increase both the professional and social network of honors students.

Members are welcome to attend our various activities such as lunch lectures, workshops, drinks, study trips and more. In addition, the association builds upon a strong Alumni network and has close ties with business and entrepreneurial things.
